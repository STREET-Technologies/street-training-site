# Your locations

**In short:** If you run more than one shop, each one is a separate collection point. You set up your main one first, and turn the others on when you are ready.

## How it works

- During setup you choose your main location, the one riders collect from.
- Your other locations are already known to STREET, but they are not live until you switch them on.
- You manage and activate locations from the STREET Partner app.
