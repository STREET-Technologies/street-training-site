# Finishing your setup

**In short:** A short four-step form: confirm your store details, set a password, set your opening hours, done.

## The four steps

1. **Confirm your store details.** Name, contact, the address riders collect from, your business category. Most of this is filled in from Shopify already, so it is mostly a check-and-fix.
2. **Set your password.** A temporary password arrives by email. Use it once to set your own.
3. **Set your operating hours.** The hours you can pack and hand over orders. You can copy one day across the week, or skip this and set it later in the app.
4. **Done.** You are ready to choose your products and take orders.

## Good to know

- Your progress is saved in the same browser as you go, so you can step away and come back.
- If you run more than one shop, you pick which location to set up here (see [[Your-Locations]]).

> Worth knowing: the temporary-password email is needed for step two, so keep it to hand. Operating hours are not set in stone, you can change them any time in the Partner app.
