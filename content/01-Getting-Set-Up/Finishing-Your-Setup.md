# Finishing your setup

**In short:** A short four-step form: confirm your store details, set a password, set your opening hours, done.

## The four steps

1. **Confirm your store details.** Name, contact, the address we collect orders from, your business category. Most of this is filled in from Shopify already. Check it and fix anything wrong.
2. **Set your password.** You get a temporary password by email. Use it once to set your own.
3. **Set your operating hours.** The hours you can actually pack and hand over orders. You can copy one day to all days, or skip and set them later in the app.
4. **Done.** You are ready to choose your products and take orders.

## Good to know

- Your progress is saved in the same browser as you go, so you can come back to it.
- If you run more than one shop, you pick which location to set up here (see [[Your-Locations]]).
