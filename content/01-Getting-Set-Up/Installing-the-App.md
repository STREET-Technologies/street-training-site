# Installing the STREET app

**In short:** Install the STREET app from the Shopify App Store, approve the access it asks for, and Shopify brings you through to finish your setup.

## What happens

- Install STREET from the Shopify App Store, the same as any other app.
- Approve the access STREET asks for. It is what lets STREET show your products to customers and send the orders back to you (see [[How-STREET-Connects-To-Your-Store]]).
- Shopify then brings you to the STREET setup to confirm your details and hours (see [[Finishing-Your-Setup]]).

## You are not live yet

Installing the app does not switch you on. You still choose your products, and your shop has to be open on STREET first, so you decide when you start appearing (see [[Getting-Visible-On-STREET]]).

> Worth knowing: the access you approve is read-and-send, not control of your shop. STREET reads your products and stock and sends you orders. It does not change how your store runs.
