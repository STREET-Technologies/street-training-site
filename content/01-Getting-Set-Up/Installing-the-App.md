# Installing the STREET app

**In short:** Install the STREET app on your Shopify store, approve the permissions, and you land in the setup portal to finish.

## Steps

- Install STREET from the Shopify App Store.
- Approve the permissions Shopify asks for. STREET needs these to see your products and send you orders.
- Shopify sends you to the STREET setup portal to finish (see [[Finishing-Your-Setup]]).
