# Choosing your products

**In short:** You pick which products appear on STREET from inside your Shopify admin, using the STREET app there. Not in the setup portal.

## How to do it

- Open your Shopify admin.
- Open the STREET app inside it.
- Toggle which products are visible on STREET. You can do this in bulk.

## What makes a product sell on STREET

- Clear, good-quality images.
- Accurate stock levels, so customers never order something you cannot fulfil.
- Correct variants (size, colour) so the customer gets exactly what they picked.

> Watch out: this is the step new retailers most often miss. If your setup is finished but nothing is live on STREET, you have probably not chosen your products here yet. It happens in Shopify admin, not the setup portal.
