# Choosing your products

**In short:** You pick which products appear on STREET from inside your Shopify admin, using the STREET app there, not in the setup form.

## How to do it

- Open your Shopify admin.
- Open the STREET app inside it.
- Choose which products appear on STREET. You can do this in bulk.

## What helps a product sell

- Clear, good photos.
- Accurate stock, so customers never order something that has gone.
- Correct variants, so the customer gets exactly the size or colour they picked.

You already do all of this for your own store, so there is nothing new to learn here.

> Watch out: this is the step new retailers most often miss. If setup is done but nothing is live on STREET, you have most likely not chosen your products yet. It happens in your Shopify admin, not the setup form. See [[Getting-Visible-On-STREET]] for the full checklist.
