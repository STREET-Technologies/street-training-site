# Welcome to STREET

**In short:** STREET sends nearby customers to your store and gets their order delivered the same day. You pack it, a rider collects it, the customer gets it in hours.

## How it works

A customer browses STREET, finds your products, and orders. The order lands with you. You accept it, pack it, and mark it ready. A STREET rider collects from your store and delivers it to the customer that same day.

You keep using Shopify the way you already do. STREET sits alongside it.

The flow, start to finish:

> Customer orders on STREET → order arrives in your STREET Partner app → you accept and pack → rider collects → delivered

## What you need to do

1. Finish setting up your store (a few minutes, covered in [[Finishing-Your-Setup]]).
2. Choose which of your products appear on STREET (see [[Choosing-Your-Products]]).
3. Accept and pack orders as they come in (see [[Receiving-Orders]]).
