# Going-live checklist

**In short:** Tick these off and you are ready to take your first STREET order.

## Before your first order

- [ ] STREET app installed on your Shopify store (see [[Installing-the-App]]).
- [ ] Store setup finished in the portal: store details, password set, operating hours (see [[Finishing-Your-Setup]]).
- [ ] Products chosen for STREET in your Shopify admin (see [[Choosing-Your-Products]]).
- [ ] Stock levels accurate in Shopify.
- [ ] Operating hours match when you can actually pack and hand over orders.
- [ ] You can log in to the STREET Partner app on the device you will use day to day.
- [ ] Your locations are set up if you run more than one shop (see [[Your-Locations]]).

## Worth doing early

- [ ] Read [[Accepting-Orders]] so the acceptance window is no surprise.
- [ ] Skim [[Returns-and-Refunds]] so your first return is calm, not a panic.
- [ ] Save the support contact from [[Get-Help]].

> Watch out: choosing your products happens in your Shopify admin, not in the setup portal. If you finished setup but see nothing live, this is almost always the missing step.
