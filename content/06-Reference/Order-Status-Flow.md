# Order status flow

**In short:** The stages an order moves through, from the moment a customer places it to delivered.

## The stages

1. **New** — a customer has placed the order. It is waiting for you to accept.
2. **Accepted** — you have taken the order and are packing it.
3. **Ready** — packed and waiting for the rider to collect.
4. **Collected** — the rider has it and is on the way.
5. **Delivered** — the customer has the order.
