# Glossary

**In short:** Plain definitions of the words you will see around STREET.

- **STREET Partner app** — the app where you accept orders, pack them, mark them ready, and manage your hours and locations.
- **Setup portal** — the one-time place you finish your store setup after installing the app.
- **Acceptance window** — the time you have to accept a new order before it can be offered elsewhere.
- **Operating hours** — the hours you have told STREET you can pack and hand over orders.
- **Location (outlet)** — one of your shops that STREET can collect orders from.
- **STREET catalogue** — the products you have made visible on STREET, chosen in your Shopify admin.
- **Rider** — the courier who collects an order from you and delivers it to the customer.
- **Delivery fee** — the cost of the rider for an order. It is not your earnings. See [[How-Billing-Works]].
- **Commission** — STREET's share of a STREET sale.
- **Cap** — the limit on how much can be billed through Shopify before an alert. See [[Caps-and-Alerts]].
