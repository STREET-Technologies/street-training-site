# FAQ

**In short:** Quick answers to the questions retailers ask most.

**I finished setup but nothing is live on STREET. Why?**
You almost certainly have not chosen your products yet. That happens in your Shopify admin, not the setup portal. See [[Choosing-Your-Products]].

**Where do I accept orders?**
In the STREET Partner app. See [[Receiving-Orders]].

**A customer is returning an item. Do I refund the delivery fee?**
No, not by default, and you should not tick "Refund shipping" just to clear a warning. See [[Returns-and-Refunds]].

**Can I change my opening hours?**
Yes, any time, in the Partner app.

**I have more than one shop. Do I set them all up at once?**
You set up your main one first. Your other locations turn on when you are ready. See [[Your-Locations]].
