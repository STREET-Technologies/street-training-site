# Customer service

**In short:** Be quick, be honest, and know when to bring STREET in. This page sets the expectations for talking to STREET customers.

## The basics

- Respond promptly. STREET customers expect the same speed in answers as in delivery.
- Be clear and friendly. You are representing both your shop and STREET.
- If something goes wrong, tell the customer what is happening rather than going quiet.

## When to bring STREET in

- For anything about the delivery itself or a problem you cannot resolve, escalate to STREET support (see [[Get-Help]]).
