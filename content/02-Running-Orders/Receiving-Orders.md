# Receiving orders

**In short:** New STREET orders arrive in the STREET Partner app. That is where you accept and work them.

## Where orders show up

- The STREET Partner app is your home for live orders.
- The order also appears in your Shopify admin, as any order would.
- Work the order from the Partner app, where the accept, pack, and ready steps live.
