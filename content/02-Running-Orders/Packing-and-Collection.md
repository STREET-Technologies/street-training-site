# Packing and collection

**In short:** Pack the order, mark it ready in the Partner app, and a STREET rider collects it from your store.

## The steps

1. Pack the items.
2. Mark the order ready for collection in the Partner app.
3. A rider is sent to collect from your store.
4. Hand the order to the rider.
