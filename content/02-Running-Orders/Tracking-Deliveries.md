# Tracking deliveries

**In short:** You can follow an order from accepted through to delivered in the Partner app.

## What you can see

- The order's current stage, updated as it moves.
- When the rider is on the way and when the order has been delivered.

See [[Order-Status-Flow]] for the full list of stages an order goes through.
