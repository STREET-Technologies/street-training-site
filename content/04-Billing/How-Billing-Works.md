# How billing works

**In short:** STREET charges a commission on STREET sales, and recovers the rider delivery cost, both through Shopify's billing. You do not handle any separate STREET invoice.

## What you are charged for

- **Commission** on the orders STREET brings you.
- **Delivery cost** for the rider on each STREET order. This is the fee that appears in your takings but is really the cost of delivery (this is the same fee covered in [[Returns-and-Refunds]]).

## Where it appears

- Charges come through Shopify's billing, so they sit alongside your normal Shopify costs.
