# Caps and alerts

**In short:** There is a cap on how much can be billed through Shopify, and you get a heads-up as you approach it. This page explains what to do when you see that alert.

## What to expect

- Shopify notifies you when billing is getting close to the cap.
- This is normal and means STREET is sending you steady business.
